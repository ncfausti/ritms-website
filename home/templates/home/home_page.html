About

The Rutgers Institute for Translational Medicine & Research (RITMS) supports research at the interface of basic and clinical research focusing on developing new and safer therapeutics. RITMS includes faculty and basic research space. RITMS also offers research cores, educational programs, and research programs and centers. These are designed to facilitate training and research particularly from proof of concept in cellular and animal model systems across the translational divide to proof of concept and dose selection in humans.
